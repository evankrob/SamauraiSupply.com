The Hanwei Paul Chen Tori XL Light Katana combines truly elegant furniture and a powder-steel folded blade with the functionality of our Performance Series. The Tori XL Light features a ”Flying Crane” themed Tsuba and Fuchi/Kashira while the Menuki are Kabuto or Japanese helmets. The high quality brown leather Tsuka-ito is wrapped over black Same. The Saya is wrapped at the mouth with black-lacquered rattan and then finished in a textured brown. The Tori XL Light is available in three folded steel cutting blade profiles and three Iaito blade lengths.
 
Competitive Cutting / Lighter Weight
The XL Light blades feature the same geometry as those of the XL but incorporate deeply cut grooves (bo-hi) to reduce the weight of the blade while retaining most of its strength and cutting ability. This weight reduction makes for a quicker sword, well adapted for multiple cuts in lighter targets.

  • New Xl Geometry with competition cutting in mind
  • Iron tsuba with crane motif
  • Brown Suede-Leather handle wrap and Black rayskin underlay
  • All premium fittings
  • Wooden scabbard with Buffalo Horn fittings and brown "fingerprintless" speckled finish
  • "Clamshell" holding area on scabbard
  • Comes with Sword bag and Premium Maintenance kit
