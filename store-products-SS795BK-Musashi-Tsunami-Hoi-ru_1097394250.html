We had the pleasure of giving the Musashi Tsunami Hoi-ru its name and if you take a look at the picture you can clearly see why. The tsuba looks like a captains wheel from an old sail ship or pirate ship. Well, Hoi-ru is Japanese for wheel. The fuchi and kashira boast a stormy wave pattern, so that is why we chose tsunami. Throw it together and you've got the Musashi Tsunami Hoi-ru Katana. Check out the video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZmcfS4A2Hyk

The Tsunami Hoi-ru has a beautiful hamon and such a great balance. If you know who Professor Gary Lee is, then you will know that he is a worldwide known sword drawer and has been dubbed the "Japanese Waterman" for his draw. He loves how the Tsunami Hoi-ru feels, and how it draws so much that he now has one in his personal collection.
