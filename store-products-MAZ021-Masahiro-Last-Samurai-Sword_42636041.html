Finally a faithful reproduction of Captain Nathan Algren's sword from The Last Samurai is here. Now you can own a hand forged, fully functional exact replica of Captain Algren's Tachi that was made for him in the movie the Last Samurai. This Samurai Sword is made by the Masahiro forge and no detail has been left out including the engraving of the samurai spirit on the side of the blade.

Masahiro Last Samurai Sword Features:

  • Hand forged HCS 1065 high carbon steel blade
  • Made in the Shinogi Zukui shape
  • Tri polished mirror finish on Shinogi-ji
  • Japanese engraving on blade (Since whose serve with dignity and honor)
  • Tsuka features Japanese silk and authentic rayskin wrap
  • Black lacquered wood saya with sageo and leather tachi sling
  • Last Samurai tsuba
  • Comes with cotton sword bag
  • An authentic replica of Nathan Algren sword from Last Samurai
  • Fully Functional
Measurements: Blade: 28", Tsuka: 10.5", Overall: 40", Weight: 2.5 Lbs
