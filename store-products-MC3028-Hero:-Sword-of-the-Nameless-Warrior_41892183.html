Finally, they have made this awesome replica from an amazing martial arts movie! The Hero Sword, with it's trademark slotted blade is made of top grade 440 stainless double edged steel. It has 2 chinese characters engraved on the 25 inch blade which say " Ean Shong" meaning Hero. Brown leather wrapped handle with leather strapping on the black wooden scabbard. Antiqued iron on guard, pommel, throat, and end of scabbard add amazing appeal to this, the sword of the Hero! Also, comes with Hero collectors headband!

Hero Sword of the Nameless Warrior Features:

  • 440 stainless steel double edged slotted blade
  • Chinese characters "hero" engraved in blade
  • Brown leather wrapped handle
  • Black Wooden scabbard with brown leather wrap
  • Comes with Hero Headband!
