About Samurai Supply

Samurai Supply opened in 2009 as a specialty online sword shop, carrying functional and decorative Japanese-style swords from forges like Musashi, Hanwei Paul Chen, Ryumon, Ten Ryu, Masahiro, Kawashima, and Thaitsuki, along with bokken, kendo equipment, hakama, tsuba, and sword care supplies.

The store itself has since closed — but rather than let a decade of product research disappear, we've preserved the entire catalog as a reference archive. Every listing keeps its original specifications, measurements, and photographs.

Because these exact production runs come and go, each page now links to a live Amazon search for the item so you can check current availability and pricing. Links to Amazon are affiliate links: as an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases, at no additional cost to you. That's what keeps this archive online.
