Tsubas


The Tsuba is usually a round or occasionally squarish guard at the end of the grip of bladed Japanese weapons, like the Katana Sword and its various declinations (Tachi, etc.), WakisashiTanto, or Naginata. They contribute to the control of the arm (the right index of the fighter typically touches the tsuba), and to the protection of the hand. Tsubas are usually finely decorated, and nowadays are collectors' items. We hope you find the Tsuba that best fits your Samurai Sword.
