This Bokken is actually an Iaido practice blade, semi-thin blade which fits into the included wooden saya. This is a MUST for anyone wanting to practice Iaido safely without a live blade. The Iaido Bokken is a great alternative to the standard Bokken, which generally does not have a saya. Since Iaido is the Art of the Draw, the Iaido Bokken advantage of having the saya gives students a better understanding of drawing principles as well as practicing noto, the motion of returning the blade to the saya.

The Iaido Bokken is made of Red Oak and has a metal kashiri/end cap as well as a cord wrap on the hilt. The Iaido Bokken is the best thing to practice Iaido or Kenjutsu with in place of a live blade or a spring steel practice blade.
