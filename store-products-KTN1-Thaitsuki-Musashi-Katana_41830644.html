Thaitsuki Musashi Katana

Thaitsuki · Model KTN1 · Thaitsuki Samurai Sword

The blade of the Musashi Katana Sword (KTN1) is specially handmade and forged in the Japanese Nihonto tradition. This forging method mixed with high quality, high carbon Japanese steel makes it one of the sharpest, strongest and flexible blades around. The tsuka (handle) is genuine same' (ray skin) and the Tsuka Ito (handle wrap) is high quality black leather. The Menuki, Fuchi and Kashira (sword mounting/fittings) are all handmade with 92.5% silver. The oxidization process creates an antique look. The Thaitsuki Musashi Katana Sword comes with a black matte finished saya and silk sword bag. The Musashi Katana Sword can be completely disassembled for full maintenance. It is a fully functional Japanese katana sword, NOT a replica.


Thatsuki Musashi Katana Sword Features:



  • Hand forged high carbon steel blade

  • Hand sharpened

  • Authentic hamon (HRC 60-30)

  • Real silver Habaki, fuchi, kashira and menuki (92.5%)

  • Brass Musashi tsuba

  • Leather and rayskin wrapped tsuka

  • Double pegged

  • Black matte finish saya

  • Fully functional

  • Comes with silk sword bag
