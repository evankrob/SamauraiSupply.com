The Miaodao (Corn Leaf Saber) is another design by Sifu Adam Hsu. This graceful sword is very quick at well under 2 lbs. and the grooved high-carbon blade hisses beautifully in the cutting stroke. The grip is bound in rattan to avoid slipping. For students qualified to use a live blade, the sword is well suited to both Miaodao form and martial applications.
Hsu Corn Leaf Saber Features:

  • High carbon steel sharpened blade

  • Makes a hissing noise when swung

  • Bound rattan grip

  • Great for students of martial arts

  • Comes with wooden scabbard which has steel accents


Measurements:


Blade: 30 1/2"

Overall: 43 1/2"
