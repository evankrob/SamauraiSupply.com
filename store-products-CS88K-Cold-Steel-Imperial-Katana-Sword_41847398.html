The Cold Steel Sword company has used only traditional materials for the Japanese swords fittings. The Tsuba (guard), Fuchi (collar), and Kashira (pommel), are expertly made and finely crafted from black iron. The Habaki (blade collar), is hand made out of polished brass and each hilt is covered in Samé (ray skin) and tightly wrapped in the traditional style with black cord. A beautiful brass Menuki ( a small, ornamental sculpture inserted between the ray skin and the cord wrappings on the handle) enhances the Samurai Swords aesthetic appearance and makes for a sword that is as traditional looking as possible. All come complete with a Black Lacquered Wood Sheath with Blue-Violet cover bag. All of the Imperial Katana Swords feature fully Sharpened 1050 carbon steel blades, where each are heat treated to a spring temper. The blade surface is then given a careful polish by hand, with a distinctive mirror polish applied to the fuller (blood groove). The Cold Steel Imperial Katana Sword is quite an impressive and unique sword and a wonder to add to your collection. See for yourself and buy yours today!
