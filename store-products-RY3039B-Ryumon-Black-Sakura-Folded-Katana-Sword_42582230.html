RY3039B- Ryumon Black Sakura Folded Katana Sword

This amazing Ryumon Black Sakura Folded Katana Sword comes from the Longquan forge in China which has been known for making the best swords in the world. Longquan is rich in resources that are perfect for making exceptional folded Katana Swords and this is one of them!

Ryumon Black Sakura Folded Katana Sword Features:

  • It is hand forged and folded AISI 1060 carbon steel blade (45Rc - Shinogi-Ji) and (58 Rc - Ha) which is a razor sharp blade and Fully Functional Sword.
  • This Black Sakura Katana is made in the Longquan Forge in China and is a great masterpiece.
  • It has an iron tsuba and furniture set and the saya is hand lacquered black with cherry blossom insets.
  • The tsuka is wrapped in authentic same rayskin and the ito is of high quality Japanese cotton.
  • The Ryumon Black Sakura Folded Katana comes with a cleaning kit, sword bag, reference guide which explains sword care AND a certificate of authenticity which is affixed on a beautiful silk scroll!
  • The certificate you receive will verify the sword makers name, Rc hardness, blade and sword measurements, date of start and completion of the sword and is certified by the sword maker and includes his years of experience.

Measurements: Blade: 28", Tsuka: 10", Overall: 40", Weight: 3.1lbs
