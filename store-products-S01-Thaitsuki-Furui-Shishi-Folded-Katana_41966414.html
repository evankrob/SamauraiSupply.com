Thaitsuki Furui Shishi Folded Katana

Thaitsuki Samurai Swords are some of the most beautiful Katana Swords and the Thaitsuki Furui Shishi Folded Katana Sword is continuing the Thaitsuki tradition. Featuring a Hand forged and folded high carbon steel blade with 1024 layers, this is among the upper echelon for the sword collector. Pure silver fittings with a Shishi motif as well as dark leather over black same (rayskin) wrapped tsuka round out this stunning Katana Sword.

Thaitsuki Furui Shishi Folded Katana features:
Hand forged and folded high carbon steel blade (1024 layers), clay tempered and water quenched blade (HRC 60-35), hand polished with excellent hada, hand sharpened, copper tsuba, silver seppa and habaki, brass fuchi/kashira with Shishi motif, dark brown leather and black same (rayskin) wrapped tsuka, dark brown matte finish saya with silver kurikata, brown silk sageo and silver sayajiri, fully functional, comes with silk sword bag and certificate.

Measurements: Blade: 29 1/2", tsuka: 11", overall: 41 1/2", weight: 2.6 Lbs
