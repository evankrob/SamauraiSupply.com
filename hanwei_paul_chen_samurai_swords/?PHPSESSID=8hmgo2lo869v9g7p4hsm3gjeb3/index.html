Hanwei Paul Chen Swords

The name Paul Chen has become synonymous with quality Samurai Swords. Paul Chen Swords are uncompromised in fit and finish and each Katana Sword is a functional and traditional piece that you can use and own with pride. "Hanwei" is the name for the Dalian Forge in China where Paul Chen produces his work. Paul Chen's Samurai Swords are respected for being reasonably priced for high quality Katana Swords.
