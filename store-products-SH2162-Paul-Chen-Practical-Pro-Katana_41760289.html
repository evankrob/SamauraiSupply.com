Paul Chen Practical Pro Katana

The Hanwei Paul Chen Practical Pro Katana is built as an economical cutting sword for heavy targets, with an extended tsuka and a thicker and longer blade. The blade of the Practical Pro Katana is constructed from hand-forged, high-carbon steel and are differentially hardened using a traditional claying method. The Practical Pro has a fine polish to it compared to others in the series. The tsuka is wrapped with synthetic black leather for its gripping characteristics and is double-pegged for safety. The saya is done in a deep black lacquer with a black cotton sageo. The katana is stylized with a round Kuruma tsuba, which represents the Wheel of Dharma, symbolizing the Buddhist concept of the Noble Eightfold Path.

This Katana Sword is a reproduction (not Licensed) of the Katana Morpheus used in the Matrix Reloaded.


Paul Chen Practical Pro Katana Sword Features:



  • Hand-Forged High Carbon Steel Blade

  • Prominent Hamon - differentially tempered

  • Nice polish

  • Fully sharpened

  • Double pegged

  • Black synthetic leather and faux same Tsuka-ito

  • Black iron koshirae

  • Comes with black sword bag
