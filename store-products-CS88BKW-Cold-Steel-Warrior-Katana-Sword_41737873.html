The Cold Steel Warrior Series swords are very handsome in their own right and share the same steel, heat treatment, and sharpness as their more expensive Imperial counterparts. Like the Imperial swords, they have Same covered, cord wrapped handles and quality fittings. If you're in the market for a strong, beautiful looking, reasonably priced Japanese Sword we know you'll be happy to own one of these as Cold Steel produce an amazing sword.

Cold Steel Warrior Katana Features:



  • Hand forged 1055 Carbon Steel Blade with bo-hi

  • Big kissaki

  • Fully sharpened

  • Cotton cord and rayskin wrapped tsuka

  • Brass menuki

  • Black lacquered wood saya

  • Fully functional - a great cutting blade

  • Comes with Blue sword bag

Blade: 29 1/4", Tsuka: 11 1/4", Overall: 40 1/2", Weight: 2.5 Lbs
