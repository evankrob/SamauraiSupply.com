Thaitsuki Kanshiki Wakizashi Sword Features:


A hand forged and clay tempered high carbon steel blade with partial bo-hi. The Thaitsuki Sword has a prominent hamon (HRC 45-55) and it is fully sharpened and fully functional. It has an antiqued blackened brass tsuba and a golden brown Japanese silk tsuka ito and black rayskin wrapped tsuka. It features real silver menuki, and a lacquered saya with golden brown woven Japanese silk sageo. This beautiful Thaitsuki sword comes with a sword bag and a certificate of authenticity.


Measurements:


Blade: 20 Tsuka: 8 ¼ Overall: 29 ¼ Weight 1 1/2lbs
